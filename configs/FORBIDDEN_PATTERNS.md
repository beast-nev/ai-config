# Forbidden Patterns

- Do not add dependencies without a clear need.
- Do not change unrelated files.
- Do not weaken validation, auth, permissions, or error handling.
- Do not hide failures with broad catches, fallbacks, or ignored errors.
- Do not invent requirements, APIs, data, or test results.
- Do not edit generated files or changelogs manually.
- Do not use destructive commands without explicit approval.
